Exam results templates

To Add , Delete or Edit Exam results templates :

 

1) Go to "Settings->Quiz result templates".

2) To Add new template click "New template" link.

3) Enter Name , Description , Success template and Fail template.You can find the list of variables, you can use it and they will be replaced with real values.

 

 

4) Click "Save" button .

 

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

All information about exam templates are storing in "result_templates" and "result_template_contents" .

 

All rows that marked as "System row" cannot be deleted .

 

Template_type =1 - Success template

Template_type =2 - Fail template
