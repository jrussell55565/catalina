Manage Exams

To Manage exams , go Assignments->Assignments .

 

You will see the list of exams and survey that will be retrived from "assignments" table of your database .

 

Just after creating an online exam, the status of exam will be "Not started" and it will not be visible to users .

You will need to press "Start" button .

 

Click start button , and this exam will be available all to all assigned users .

 

After you started exam , you can stop it . When you click "Stop" button , it stops exam and calculates points for all users .

 

You will not be able to "Restart" it after stopped . To Start it again you need to press "Reset" button , but in this way , all results will be deleted .

 

During the exam , you will be able to send notifications to users , add or remove users from exam and see which questions they have answered already . To do this , you need to click "Information link ".

 

After you clicked "Information" link you will the all entered information about this exam and all assigned users .

 

Before exam started , or even after started you can send "Start mail notification" to users that you want . Just click checkboxes and press "Send start mail " button at the bottom of page.

 

After exam finished , you can send results by email . To do that , click "Send results mail" button .

 

To send notifications during the exam to the screens of users , click "notifications" link at the bottom of page .

 

Enter Subject and body and click "Send" button .

 

 

To add users during the exam , select the user and click "Add" button .

 

 

To remove user during the exam , just click "Remove" button .

 

 

To view user's results click "Details" link . You can also modify user results from this page.

 

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

What happens in backend ?

 

When you click "start" button , it changes the status of assignment from 0 to 1 . Just running one update command like this : update assignments set status = 1 where id = ASG_ID

 

When you click "stop" button, it changes the status of assignment from 1 to 2 .

 

When you click "Reset" button , it changes the assignment status to 0 . And removes data of this assignment from "user_quizzes" and "user_answers" table .  

 

When you click "Delete" button it removes the rows from below listed tables

1) assignments

2) user_quizzes

3) user_answers

4) mailed_users

5) variant_quizzes

 

When you click "Remove" button , to remove user from the exam , it removes data from "assignment_users" and "user_quizzes" table .

 

When you click "Add user" button , it's just adding user_id to assignment_users table .

 

When you click "Send start mail " and "Send results mail" it send the mail and add a row to "mailed_users" table .

 

When you add notification , it's inserting a row to "nots" table .
