Integration with facebook

 

1) Open developers.facebook.com

2) Click Apps-Register as a Developer

 

 

3) Enter you phone number and confirm registration

 

 

4) Click Apps->Create a new app

 

 

5) Enter you application name and click "Create" button

 

 

6) Click "Settings" and then click to Add Platform button , and select web site .

 

 

7) Click "Settings"   and enter your e-mail address and site URL

 

 

8) Click Migrations tab and turn on this setting

 

 

9) Click Status & Review and turn on application

 

 

10) During the installation of Php Web Quiz , it will ask you to enter the Facebook application ID , and App Secret , you will need to take from Dashboard , as below

 

 

 

11) Enter App ID and App Secret into config.php file

 

define("FACEBOOK_INTEGRATE","yes");   // "no" = all facebook features will be disabled . "yes" = fb functions will be enabled   define("FACEBOOK_APP_ID","app code");   // id of your facebook application  

define("FACEBOOK_SECRET","app secrey");   // secret of your facebook application
