Starting Exam

All users that are in "Users" role , by default will be redirected to "Active assignment" page .

They will be able to see all Started exams in this page .

 

By clicking "Start" button user will be redirect to the exam page.

 

At the left top of the page , there is Timer . At the right side you can see the list of questions .

 

 

Hover mouse over the numbers , and you will see the tooltip

 

 

Click numbers to go to the clicked question . But in this way the results will not be saved . The only way to save answered question is to click "Save & Next " button .

 

When you come to the last question you will see "Finish" button . By clicking to this button you will finish exam .

 

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

What happens in backend ?

 

When user clicks "start" , it add a new row into "user_quizzes" table .

 

 

When user clicks " Save & Next" button , it saves the answers in "user_answers" table

 

 

 

Also , it saves the point for each question in "assignment_question_points"

 

 

To calculate point of question it uses stored procedure "p_insert_question_point".

 

When user clicks "Finish" button , it calls procedure "p_calc_quiz_results" .   This procedure calculates total point of exam , and save results into the table "user_quizzes".
