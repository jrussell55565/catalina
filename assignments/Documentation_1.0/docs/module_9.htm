Email templates

To configurate email templates :

 

1) Go to "Settings -> Email Templates "

2) Select Email template that you want to modify .

3) Change mail text . You can find the list of variables that you can use in mail text and which will be replaced with real values.

 

4) Click "Save" button .

 

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

mail templates are storing in "email_templates" table
